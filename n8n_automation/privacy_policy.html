Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 29, 2025

1. Introduction

N8N Content Scheduler ("we", "our", or "us") is an automation tool that helps users schedule and publish content across multiple social media platforms including YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok.

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

Our application collects and processes the following information:

  • Authentication Tokens: Access tokens provided by social media platforms (YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, TikTok) to publish content on your behalf
  • Content Data: Videos, images, captions, and metadata that you choose to publish through our service
  • Account Information: Your social media account identifiers necessary for content publishing

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Publish your content to the social media platforms you have authorized
  • Execute scheduled posting tasks as configured by you
  • Maintain the connection between our service and your social media accounts

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Access tokens are stored securely and encrypted
  • Content is processed only for the purpose of publishing and is not permanently stored
  • We do not sell, trade, or transfer your information to third parties

5. Data Sharing

Your information is shared only with:

  • Social Media Platforms: YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok receive only the content and metadata you choose to publish through their official APIs
  • We do not share your data with any other third parties for marketing or advertising purposes

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke access to your social media accounts at any time through the respective platform settings
  • Request deletion of your authentication data by contacting us
  • Access information about what data we process on your behalf

7. Data Retention

We retain your access tokens only for as long as necessary to provide the service. When you disconnect your accounts or stop using our service, authentication data is removed.

8. Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with the following platforms:

  • YouTube: Subject to Google's Privacy Policy and YouTube Terms of Service
  • Facebook/Instagram: Subject to Meta's Data Policy and Terms of Service
  • TikTok: Subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service

We recommend reviewing each platform's privacy policies to understand how they handle your data.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

11. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.
